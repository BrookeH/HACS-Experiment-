Group 2 Experiment

Requirements

You must be a native english speaker.

You need to be a student or employee of York University to take part in this experiment

Introduction

Once you sign and click continue, you will be undertaking a reading task comprising of 2 texts and a short reading comprehension after each one you will be asked several questions related to the text. If you are unsure of the answer to any of the questions related to the text just guess. We expect the overall task to take less than 10 minutes.

To ensure that our experiment is valid

  • Please keep your phone's brightness constant
  • Refrain from moving into a different environment such as another room.
  • Please avoid any possible distractions such as music around you for the duration of the experiment.
  • We request that you be in a state of best vision during the experiment thus if you require glasses, please wear them during the experiment
  • Please DO NOT use the back buttons to go back to previous pages or any other buttons on your device other than the buttons we provide on the web page
  • Please remain focused during the experiment and do not reply to emails or text inbetween questions. If you can please put you phone into do not disturb mode to avoid these distractions.

All of the data we collect and you enter is stored securely and anonymously. All personal questions are optional or have the option of prefer not to say. You may withdraw from the experiment at any point. You may request to have your data erased at any point during or after the experiment by sending an email to vsv501@york.ac.uk and quoting your ID which is listed at the top of this page, the final page and the debried page. Once you agree to proceed, information on the type of device you are using (whether it is a smartphone, desktop pc or tablet) will be obtained automatically. Some data may be collected in the background whilst you perform the experiment, you will be told what this is at the end of the experiment and you will be given the option to have the data erased then.

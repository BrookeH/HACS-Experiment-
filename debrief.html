Debrief

Thank you for participating in our experiment, we greatly appreciate the data you have given us.

This experiment was all about line spacing. Previous studies we looked at relating to line spacing have found that 1.5 or 2.0 line spacing is prefered by all ages, however these studies have been conducted within the limits of a lab and using desktop PC's.

Our aim was to observe the effects that line spacing had on your preference and understanding of text and whether using a mobile device plays any part in the decision.

Our hypothesis is that 1.5 line spacing will be prefered by all age groups, over the 1.0 line spacing regardless of the device used.
